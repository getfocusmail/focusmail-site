How It Works

From cluttered inbox to clear priorities in seconds

1
Install the extension

Add FocusMail to Chrome in one click from the Chrome Web Store. No account or sign-up required.

2
Click "Refresh"

FocusMail scans your recent emails and analyses each one for urgency signals, scam indicators, and action items.

3
See your priorities instantly

Emails appear ranked across four tabs — All, Urgent, Soon, and Low — with scam-flagged emails surfaced for your review.

4
Check them off as you go

Tick each email as you handle it. Switch to the To-do list or Weekly Calendar view for a focused, time-aware task list.
